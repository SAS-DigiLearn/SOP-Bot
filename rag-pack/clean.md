# Scottish Ambulance Service – Management of Rest Breaks (SOP, Test of Change v1.0)

## Aim
To ensure appropriate and effective rest break management... “nobody should work more than 6 hours without a break.”

## Definition of a Rest Break
A short period not less than 20 mins, undisturbed, not at the start or end of the day, allowing an employee to stop and relax…

## Application
- Dispatchers within ACCs are responsible for consistent application.
- Applies to all operational colleagues booked onto the C3 system.

## Policy
The Service gives an absolute commitment to provide rest breaks… SOP supports Dispatchers to balance colleague welfare and patient safety…

## Rest Break Windows
- 8-hour shift: window 3rd–5th hour, 30 minutes
- 10-hour shift: window 4th–6th hour, 38 minutes
- 12-hour shift: two breaks (25 + 20), windows 3rd–5th and 7th–9th hour
